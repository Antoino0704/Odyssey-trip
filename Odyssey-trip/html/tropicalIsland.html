Odyssey-trip

On the first day, our guide will give you a tour of the local historical site, a map of the place, and show the various places. You will be able to see various temples, walk around the city and see the statues that represented the various idols of the time. After that, our guide will take you to the center of the city and show you a large fountain with a statue of a lion on top (an animal loved by the locals). On the second day, we will take you to the local botanical garden. As soon as you will enter, you will immediately notice the variety of local flora, full of different colors and shapes. You will be able to see very old, house-sized trees, tons of brightly colored flowers, and plants of all kinds. On the third day we will take you to a forest and, thanks to the equipment we will provide, you will be able to camp with the help of our tour guide and he will explain how to set up a tent to sleep as comfortably as possible.

Information:

  • Cost per person: $570
  • Duration 3 days
  • Maximum number of participants: 3
